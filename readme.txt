WICE Contact for WordPress connects your WordPress-Blog with the WICE CRM-Groupware.

== Description ==

With the WICE Contact for WordPress you can build a contact form. Just put the simple tag [contact] in your article and the contact form is shown on your blog.
Th Plugin will send the data to from your WordPress-Blog to the WICE CRM-Groupware. The WICE CRM-Groupware is a web-based solution for customer relationship management. The WICE CRM-Groupware automatically adds the new address and contactperson and starts a new ticket for further contact management.

== Installation ==

1. Upload the directory 'hs_wice_contact' to the '/wp-content/plugins/' directory
2. Activate the plugin through the 'Plugins' menu in WordPress
3. Go to newly entry 'WICE Contact' in the 'Admin' menu in WordPress
4. Build your contact form
5. Put the tag [contact] in a new article
